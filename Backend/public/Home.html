Welcome to Quantum!


STORY: In Quantum we find Agent Cooper on a secret NASA mission with the goal of finding a planet to migrate to as the Earth has its days numbered. What happens is that when he arrives on the planet Gargantúa he suffers an accident with his ship and with the explosion finds himself at Mann's planet. In order to be able to return to Earth, you have to overcome several planets with an increasing difficulty.
